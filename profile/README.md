# ON1 Photo RAW – Professional Photo Editing & Raw Processing Software

## About ON1 Photo RAW
ON1 Photo RAW is a professional-grade photo editing software designed for photographers seeking a complete workflow solution.  
It combines powerful raw processing, advanced layering, precise masking, and high-performance digital asset management into a single application.  
ON1 Photo RAW provides studio-level control over color grading, retouching, and detail enhancement, making it an essential tool for both enthusiasts and professionals who demand creative freedom and speed.

---

## Getting Started

1. Download ON1 Photo RAW and extract the archive.  
2. Launch the application directly from the folder (no installation needed).  
3. Log in via email, QR code, or existing session.  
4. Adjust interface settings, appearance, and catalog preferences.  
5. (Optional) Copy the portable folder to an external drive for mobility.

## Key Features
🎨 **Advanced Raw Processing** – Non-destructive raw editing with exceptional detail recovery.  
🖌️ **Precision Masking & Layers** – Professional layering and AI-powered masking tools.  
📸 **Digital Asset Management** – Organize and manage your photography library efficiently.  
🌈 **Color Grading Tools** – Complete control over color, tone, and creative effects.  
✍️ **Retouching & Healing** – Advanced retouching brushes for flawless photo editing.  
🖼️ **High-Performance Workflow** – Optimized for speed with large catalogs and batch processing.  
📁 **File Compatibility** – Supports raw formats from major camera brands, PSD, TIFF, JPEG, and PNG.
